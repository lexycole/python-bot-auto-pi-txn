Information & Warnings

Security Warning

Never share your secret key with anyone. This application processes your key locally and does not store or transmit it to any external server. However, for maximum security, we recommend using this tool only with wallets containing funds you're willing to risk.

Usage Guide
  1. Enter your Pi wallet's secret key and public address
  2. Enter the receiver's Pi wallet address
  3. Set the amount to send per transaction
  4. Click "Connect Wallet" to verify and get your balance
  5. Click "Start Auto-Sending" to begin the transaction bot
  6. Monitor transaction history and stop when desired
